About Us

About Chapman

Chapman's personalized education gives each student a unique opportunity to become an original thinker who rises to the world’s challenges. We’re here to make something: films, business models, lines of code—to bring them to life and deliver them to the world.

Our inclusive, empowering community fosters creative thinking and expression of all perspectives. Chapman’s campus is the perfect size—not too big, not too small—allowing for effective, innovative programs to be a focal point of our institution. We work to connect students to faculty and the community to keep students engaged in the larger world. We're here to see what we can do. So when we have finally reached where we've worked so hard to be, we'll have every experience at Chapman inside us. Every person we found inspirational behind us. Every lesson we learned within us.

All our course and program ranging from degree, master's and post-graduate are highly accredited and register in the educational body Authorize At Chapman we are proud of our conducive classrooms, well-equipped Science and Language laboratories, robust e-library facilities, quality ICT center, driven by our highly disciplined and dedicated staff to enable teaching, learning, research and community service blossom. We host multiple seminars, retreats and conferences in our peaceful and vibrant academic environment.
